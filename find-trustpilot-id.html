Enter a company's domain or Trustpilot URL to find their Business Unit ID

How to use:

  1. Enter either:
    • A company domain (e.g., "example.com")
    • A Trustpilot review URL (e.g., "trustpilot.com/review/example.com")
  2. Click "Find Business Unit ID"
  3. The tool will fetch the business information from Trustpilot's public API
  4. Copy the Business Unit ID to use in your Trustpilot widgets

Common examples to try:

  • watt.co.uk
  • amazon.co.uk
  • trustpilot.com
  • netflix.com
